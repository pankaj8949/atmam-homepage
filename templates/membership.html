Membership at Atmam Edutech Private Limited

Join a Thriving Research & Academic Community

Atmam Edutech Private Limited is committed to fostering excellence in research, education, and professional development. We offer two types of membership—Annual Membership and Lifetime Membership—each providing a range of exclusive benefits related to publication, international conferences, and award nominations.

Lifetime Membership

  • Eligibility: Open to academicians, researchers, industry professionals, and students.
  • Validity: One time in life from the date of enrolment.
  • Fees: ₹8000.00 (Indian Applicants) / $300.00 (Non-Indian Applicants)
Publication Benefits:
  • 50% discount on publication charges for JANOLI International Journals.
  • Priority in the processing of research papers for peer review.
  • Free access to select published articles and research papers.
  • Assistance with manuscript formatting and adherence to journal guidelines.
  • Opportunity to receive expert feedback on submitted research papers before final review.
International Conference Participation:
  • 20% discount on conference registration fees for all Atmam Edutech-organized international conferences.
  • Exclusive invitations to Atmam Edutech-hosted workshops, seminars, and networking sessions.
  • Access to post-conference proceedings and recorded sessions for future reference.
  • Special recognition for the best presentations and research contributions.
Awards & Recognition:
  • Eligibility to be nominated for all annual academic awards.
  • Consideration for editorial/reviewer board member in JANOLI International Journals and Abhi International Journals.

Term Membership (5 Years)

  • Eligibility: Open to individuals who wish to work with Atmam Edutech for a fixed term.
  • Validity: Membership fee for 5 years.
  • Fees: ₹5000.00 (Indian Applicants) / $210.00 (Non-Indian Applicants)
Publication Benefits:
  • 50% discount on publication charges for JANOLI International Journals.
  • Complimentary editing and proofreading support for submitted manuscripts.
  • Dedicated research guidance for improving publication success rates.
International Conference Participation:
  • 15% discount on conference registration fees for all Atmam Edutech-organized international conferences (India/abroad).
  • Complimentary access to select Atmam Edutech workshops and networking events.
  • Opportunity to serve as a session chair or keynote speaker at Atmam Edutech conferences.
  • Personalized mentorship for research paper submissions and conference presentations.
  • Exclusive access to premium conference materials, including expert panel discussions.
Awards & Recognition:
  • Automatic consideration for awards in all categories.
  • Term members' research contributions will be featured in an annual Atmam Edutech publication showcase.
  • Name displayed on the Atmam Edutech website as a Term Member and Contributor.
Additional Membership Privileges:
  • Access to a global network of researchers and professionals.
  • Opportunities to collaborate on funded research projects.
  • Exclusive access to research grants and funding opportunities provided by Atmam Edutech.
  • Subscription to Atmam Edutech newsletters and industry reports containing the latest academic insights.
  • Priority consideration for leadership roles within Atmam Edutech committees and research groups.
