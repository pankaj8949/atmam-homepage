About Us

Empowering Education Through Innovation and Technology

At Atmam Edutech Publications Private Limited, we are dedicated to transforming the education landscape by integrating technology, innovation, and expertise. Incorporated on February 15, 2025, and recognized as a startup by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), we specialize in providing top-notch educational resources, digital solutions, and research platforms that empower learners, educators, and institutions.

Our journey began with a vision to bridge the gap between quality education and modern technology. Today, we stand at the forefront of the EduTech sector, offering a wide range of services, including web development, software solutions, research journal publications, educational training, and textbook publications. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we continue to drive educational growth by ensuring accessible and impactful learning experiences for everyone.
